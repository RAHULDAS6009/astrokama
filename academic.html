I am Suvendu, a devoted KP Astrologer who believes astrology is more than charts and predictions—it is a way to guide people toward a practical, purposeful, and fulfilling lifestyle. My faith rests above all in the Supreme Lord, whose divine grace is greater than any calculation or forecast.

Across the world, many astrologers continue to rely on traditional methods. While these ancient techniques hold wisdom, I have often felt they can be limited or misunderstood. The KP technique, with its remarkable precision in calculation, became my foundation. Yet I realized that prediction alone needed refinement. Out of this realization, I developed Modern Astrology—a unique blend of KP methods and my own innovations, designed to meet the realities of today's fast-paced world. For over 11 years, I have practiced this system, offering guidance that is accurate, practical, and deeply fulfilling.
